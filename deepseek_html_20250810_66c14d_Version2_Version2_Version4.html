About Me

Media and Communication Specialist with extensive experience in research, analysis, and education

I am a passionate media and communication professional with a PhD focus on African Migrants in Yiwu, China. My work integrates media culture, research analysis, and educational training to address complex migration and cultural communication issues.

With a multicultural background spanning China, the UK, Austria, and the US, I bring a global perspective to media analysis, research methodologies, and cross-cultural communication strategies.
